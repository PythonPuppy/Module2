Attached Macro enabled Worksheet that loops through all the stocks for one year and outputs the following information in a Summary Table 
in columns "I" ,"J", "K","L" respectively .

Column I :- The ticker symbol

Column J:- Yearly change from the opening price at the beginning of a given year to the closing price at the end of that year.

Column K:- The percentage change from the opening price at the beginning of a given year to the closing price at the end of that year.

Column L:- The total stock volume of the stock.


Also a separate tabl to show the 
1- Greatest % increase for the respective Ticker value 
2. Least % increase for the respective Ticker Value 
3. Greatest Total Volume for Ticker 


Note : Red colour is for value <0
Green for colour >0
